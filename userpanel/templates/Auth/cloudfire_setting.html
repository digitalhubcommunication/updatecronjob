How to Whitelist Our IP Addresses in Cloudflare

Cloudflare can sometimes block our servers from accessing and updating orders. Follow the steps below to whitelist IP addresses in Cloudflare.

  1. Log in to Your Cloudflare Account: Visit the Cloudflare website and log in using your email and password.
  2. Select Your Website: After logging in, click on the domain name you want to manage.
  3. Navigate to Security: On the left-hand menu, click "Security" and then "WAF."
  4. Create a Custom Rule: Under the WAF section, click "Custom Rules" and select "Create Rule."
  5. Add IP Addresses: Enter the FastCronJob IP addresses as Incoming IP Source conditions. Use "Or" between each IP. Select "Skip" as the action and check the box to skip "All remaining custom rules."
  6. Deploy the Rule: Click "Deploy" to save the changes. Ensure all current IP addresses are added.
